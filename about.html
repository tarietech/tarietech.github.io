SHORT HISTORY

After 5 years of staying in Australia the Founders being missionaries had time to talk and counsel people who lost their hard earned money through scrupulous constructors. Hence Edgar Productions (PVT) Limited was formed upon return to Zimbabwe.

Edgar Productions is a growing empire that seeks to make life easy for our clients as they are about their lives while we monitor their projects. We started as a media company covering all sorts of events and car rental, 2021 we decided to venture into property management.
